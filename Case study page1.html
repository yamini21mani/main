    GET TO BUSINESS

    Return to Work safely with Breazie


    Touch Free Sign in

    Our contactless sign in solution provides a hygienic, touch free way to register anyone entering your workplace. Visitors can easily sign in by scanning a QR code with the camera on their smartphone.

    Employee Sign in with Health Check

    Using the employee sign in feature Breazie is also an efficient employee management system. The iPad can easily be used as a digital in out board, or employee can download the dedicated app.

    Visitor and Employee Screening

    Breazie can help protect your staff and business from the risk of exposure to infectious diseases. Easily screen your visitors and employees by asking them questions.

    Digital Agreements

    Any requirement for visitors to view and sign your non-disclosure agreement, health and safety register / COVID-19 policy is easy handled, assisting as a tool in your regulatory compliance.
